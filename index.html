Dolphin are Mammels that evolved from Cetaceans (whales, dolphins, and porpoises). Dolphins are active predetors and eat a wide variety of fishes, squids, and crustaceans such as shrimps. There are 49 species of Dolphins such as the Amazon River Dolphin, The Spinner Dolphin, The Bottle-nose dolphin and burrunan dolphins (theres less then 180 of them left).
